AngliPort Has Been Officially Renamed

AngliPort (angliport.com) has officially been renamed to Learn Portuguese with Barbara Sharon (barbarasharon.com.au).

This is a permanent business rebrand. While the name and domain have changed, the teacher, services, and commitment to high-quality Portuguese instruction remain exactly the same.

New Business Name and Website

Previous name: AngliPort
Previous domain: angliport.com

New name: Learn Portuguese with Barbara Sharon
New domain: https://barbarasharon.com.au

Why the Change?

The new name better reflects the personal, professional teaching approach of Barbara Sharon and clearly communicates the focus on Portuguese language education.

Portuguese Lessons and Services

Under the new name, the same services continue to be offered:

  • Private Portuguese lessons (online worldwide and in-person in Australia)
  • Beginner and intermediate Portuguese classes
  • Conversation and speaking practice sessions
  • Personalised language learning programs
  • Online Portuguese lessons for students globally

Looking for AngliPort?

If you searched for AngliPort or visited angliport.com, you are in the right place. The business now operates exclusively under the name Learn Portuguese with Barbara Sharon at barbarasharon.com.au.
