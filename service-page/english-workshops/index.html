English Workshops & Language Sessions

Discover interactive English workshops designed to enhance language learning through engaging, hands-on activities. Led by Barbara Sharon, experienced educator and Portuguese teaching specialist.

Interactive English Workshop

Join our interactive English workshops where participants engage in dynamic, hands-on language activities. These sessions combine proven teaching methodologies from Portuguese language education with practical English communication skills development.

Group Language Sessions

Participate in collaborative group sessions that encourage peer-to-peer learning and communication practice. Our group workshops create a supportive environment where participants can develop confidence in English while benefiting from diverse perspectives and experiences.

Personalized Language Coaching

One-on-one coaching sessions tailored to your specific language learning goals. Whether you need assistance with business communication, academic English, or everyday conversation skills, receive personalized guidance and feedback from an experienced educator.

Virtual English Workshops

Access our online workshops from anywhere in the world. Using interactive digital platforms, we deliver engaging language learning experiences that maintain the energy and participation of in-person sessions. Perfect for remote learners seeking flexible scheduling without compromising quality.

Teaching Methodology

Our English workshops incorporate innovative teaching techniques including:

  • Task-based learning approaches
  • NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming) principles
  • Mind mapping for vocabulary retention
  • Cultural integration through cooking, music, and art activities
  • Body language and non-verbal communication exercises

As a certified educator with expertise in both English and Portuguese language teaching, Barbara Sharon brings a unique perspective to language education, blending proven methodologies with creative engagement strategies.
